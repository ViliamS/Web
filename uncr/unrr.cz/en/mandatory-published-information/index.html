02

Reason and way of establishment

The Office of the Czech Fiscal Council was established on the basis of Section 32 of Act No. 23/2017 Coll., on the rules of budgetary responsibility:
(1) The Office of the Council is hereby established which shall assume the tasks related to the professional, organisational, administrative, personnel and technical support of the Council’s activities. The Office of the Council is headed by the Head of the Office of the Council.
(2) The Office of the Council is the organisational unit of the state and the accounting unit. Acting on behalf of the state in labour-law relations is the Head of the Office of the Council.
(3) The Head of the Office of the Council shall be appointed and removed by the Council. The Head of the Office of the Council shall be subordinate to the Chairperson of the Council.
For more information, see part four of Act No. 23/2017 Coll., on the rules of budgetary responsibility.

Price list for providing information

The Office of the Czech Fiscal Council may, on the basis of Act No. 106/1999 Coll., require payment for the provision of information subject to the obligation to provide information. However, the amount of the payment may not exceed the material costs.

On the basis of Act No. 106/1999 Coll., as amended, the Office of the Czech Fiscal Council establishes the following price list of payments for the provided information.

Current price list of payments for the provided information
– One page printout or A4 copy of the same format         CZK 2.00
– Double side printout or A4 copy                                         CZK 3.00
– One page printout or A3 copy                                              CZK 3.50
– Double side printout or A3 copy                                         CZK 4.50
– 1 piece of blank CD for writing information                     CZK 8.00
– 1 piece of blank DVD for writing information                  CZK 10.00
– Other technical data medium                                               at the purchase price
– packaging                                      based on the purchase price of the respective type of packaging
– postage                                          based on the current postal service provider’s tariff

In case the provision of information requires extraordinary search and processing, the enquirer will pay CZK 350 for each commenced hour of work of the processor.

Prospective payments can be made to the account number 19-4726001/0710 (ČNB)

Information will be provided only following the demonstrable payment of the required costs.
